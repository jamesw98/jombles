Fencing Drill Index
Footwork
  • Distance Drill

    One fencer leads the rest of the fencers

    The group is supposed to keep distance with the leader while the leader advances and retreats

    If the leader lunges, the group waits for him to recover, then lunges and recovers

  • Italian Drill

    Each fencer has a glove

    Fencers come en guarde at advance lunge distance

    Alternating turns, the fencers use an advance lunge to attempt to hit the other

    During the attacking fencer's turn, the other may take as many retreats as they want

    After getting hit, switch attackers

  • Smooth Drill

    One fencer has a glove

    This drill can done stationary or moving

    The fencer without a glove is trying to lunge and grab the glove

    The fencer with the glove can move the glove out of the way when the other lunges

    When doing this moving, the fencer w/o the glove is trying to manipulate distance

  • Patterns

    This section is just a list of useful footwork patterns:

    Lunge >>> Recover Forward >>> Lunge >>> Recover

    Small Advance >>> Advance >>> Advance Lunge

    Small Advance >>> Advance >>> Jump Lunge (Balestra)

    Advance Lunge >>> Recover Forward >>> Jump Lunge

    2 Retreats >>> Advance Lunge >>> Recover Forward >>> Fleche

    Jump Lunge >>> Recover >>> Advance Lunge >>> Recover Forward >>> Fleche

    Advance >>> Retreat >>> Advance >>> Check Forward >>> Lunge

    Retreat >>> Advance >>> Retreat >>> Check Back >>> Fleche

Bladework
  • Get Away and Go Drill

    This is essentially a bladework version of the Smooth Drill

    Only footwork allowed is advance lunge and retreat

    Attacking fencer is allowed to advance lunge in attempt to hit the other

    The non-attacking fencer is allowed as many retreats as they want

    Alternate after the other is hit
